Kalawawa

For the smile of a deprived child

A tri-generational nonprofit organization that raises funds to provide gifts for impoverished children in the highlands of La Paz Bolivia.

Learn more

Our Mission

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to improve the living standards of some of the most less fortunate people in Latin America. Bolivia is the poorest country in South America, more than half of its population lives in extreme poverty, meaning they don't have the main living necessities such as drinkable water, healthy meals, adequate clothing and/or a suitable home. Most of these families live in rural areas in the Bolivian highlands (Altiplano) were the climate and temperature are extremely harsh, with temperatures below zero , and these people do not have access to a decent shelter and they cannot afford proper clothing.

Our Commitment

Unlike many other charity entities our foundation dedicates 100% of the money collected directly to the needy. We don't have any adminstrative overhead, we don't rent a place for our operations, we don't pay for plane tickets for the people that give the donations in Bolivia. All these tasks are done by volunteers that are ready to contribute from their own money so that every cent reaches the needy.

Our Roots

Our foundation started in our family, but everyday we see that the necesities increase and everyday there are more poor people waiting for a handout. In these six years of labor we have seen people waiting for us to recieve our small contribution that for the needy has an immense value.
